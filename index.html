My 3D Printer

My current 3D Printer is an Anet A8. I bought it in 2018 and heavily customized it since then. I would like to evolve to a Prusa MINI+ as my next 3D Printer.
  • Modeling
  • Slicing
  • Controling
Modeling my own 3D models using Blender (since 2012) and SolidWorks
Slicing and printing using Cura and PrusaSlicer
Remotely controlling and monitoring using Octoprint with a Raspberry Pi 4

3D Printer Mods

Filament Guide and Cables holder

Those 2 prints screwed on the X axis module improve filament feeding and cable organisation.

Magnetic cooling fan holder

I printed this great 2 parts magnetic cooling fan holder. It provides easier access for filament change. It needed 8 tiny magnets so the 2 parts stick well.

Printing bed adjusting screw

I printed those 4 enhanced adjusting screws to make fine tuning more confortable and precise than the orginal wing nuts. I put some oil so it rotates better.

Functional prints

Shutter crank holder

A print I did to replace a broken shutter crank holder next to a window. I needed to edit the 3D model so I didn't need to replace the original screw.

Key Replica

A replica of a box key, it needed at least 4 prototypes to be identical to the original and fully functional, I have been using it almost daily for 2 years. (green censor line for privacy)

Google Home Mini wall mount

A print I did to hold my Google Home Mini smart speaker on the wall, sticked using double-sided adhesive, added two Ikea Fixa pads for more stability

Art prints

Fall Guys Figurine

An 8cm tall figurine printed in 3 parts of different colors, sticked together, the eyes are painted using a black marker

Flower pot

I printed this fancy low poly flower pot that blends greatly with my other pots. I added a hole on the bottom on the 3D model for water evacuation.

Minecraft crafting table

I printed a 30x30mm cube to stick a papercraft model of the Minecraft crafting table around it. Thanks to this it won't get crushed anymore.
